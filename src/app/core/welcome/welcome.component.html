About Us

With Milk-Bottle you can fulfill all your dairy needs in the most Hassle-free manner.

Milk is the ‘nature’s perfect food’ for all ages. It is full of all the vital nutrients needed for growth and well being of all the human body.

In this busy modern lifestyle it’s difficult to get best quality milk and milk products We are here to bring that nature’s perfect food with best quality to your doorstep every day.

We deliver farm fresh creamy milk direct from villages/farmers to your home every day without compromising hygienic and quality and without any added preservatives.

We not only deliver milk also health and nutrition’s to your families, with our high fat milk you can make creamy butter, ghee and buttermilk.

Milking is taken in old traditional ways manually two times per day and we use traditional buffalos and cows and packed and sealed in glass bottles and will reach your home within two hours of milking.
